# 👥 HR Data Analysis Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

The HR Data Analysis Dashboard is an interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze workforce demographics, employee distribution, salary trends, education levels, and workforce composition.

The dashboard provides HR professionals and business leaders with valuable insights into employee demographics, compensation patterns, workforce diversity, and organizational structure to support data-driven human resource decisions.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

- Analyze workforce demographics.
- Monitor employee distribution across job roles.
- Evaluate salary trends and compensation patterns.
- Understand workforce diversity by gender and education level.
- Track employee hiring trends over time.
- Support strategic HR decision-making.

## 🔍 Key Insights

### Workforce Demographics
- The organization consists of 161 employees.
- The average employee age is 35.2 years.
- Male employees slightly outnumber female employees.

### Job Role Analysis
- Packaging Associate is the most common job role.
- Production Operators and Sales Representatives represent a significant portion of the workforce.
- Workforce distribution varies across departments and job functions.

### Compensation Analysis
- Product Managers receive the highest average salary.
- Employees with Master's Degrees earn the highest average salaries.
- Salary levels vary significantly across job roles.

### Workforce Trends
- Employee hiring increased significantly between 2017 and 2023.
- Workforce growth remained relatively stable in recent years.
